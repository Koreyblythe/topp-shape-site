Programs at Topp Shape

Topp Shape isn’t just a traditional gym — it’s a fitness center for those serious about personal health and growth. We combine training, nutrition, and accountability into a system that gets results.

Group Classes

High-energy group sessions designed to build strength, improve conditioning, and keep fitness engaging with a supportive community.

Athlete Training

Performance-based training for athletes of all levels. Focus on speed, agility, strength, and conditioning specific to sport demands.

1:1 Personal Training

Custom coaching tailored to your individual goals, fitness level, and lifestyle. Work directly with our trainers for results you can count on.
